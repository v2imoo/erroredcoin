What We Do

Four Services.
One Direction.

A clear progression from diagnosis to stability to recovery to disciplined re-entry. Each service does exactly one thing — and does it completely. You move forward only when you are ready.

01
Health Check
A structured panel conversation — 90 minutes — that examines your full situation. The decisions, the timeline, the money, the people, the moment when things turned. No judgment. No product waiting at the end. A complete and honest look at what actually happened, followed by a written diagnosis in plain language.
How the session works
You come with your story — however incomplete it feels. Our panel comes with pattern recognition built from years of seeing similar situations across different industries. We ask the questions you have not been asked. We follow the money trail. Within a few days, you receive a written summary: which error type occurred, what caused it, what your current position actually is, and what your realistic options look like.
Right for you if
You know something went wrong but cannot clearly explain what happened or why. You want to understand the situation before taking any action.

What you receive
  • 90-minute panel session with the relevant experts
  • Written error diagnosis — plain language, no jargon
  • Error type identified — which of the six drove the situation
  • Realistic options — what can be recovered and what must be released
  • Recommended next step — your decision to make
02
Loss Control
Before rebuilding, stop the ongoing damage. This service identifies what is currently still actively destroying value and provides a structured exit or correction plan for each. The instinct after a loss is to immediately act to recover it. The first right action is to stop making things worse. This is the emergency room before the rehabilitation ward.
What we examine
Every ongoing drain on your resources — financial and operational. A position still losing money every week. A vendor contract extracting more than it contributes. A debt structure where interest compounds faster than the business generates cash. We map each one, assess whether correction or exit is appropriate, and build a sequenced plan for stopping the bleeding.
Right for you if
The diagnosis is done and the situation is still deteriorating. You have active obligations making the problem larger every month.

What you receive
  • Active loss mapping — every drain identified and quantified
  • Debt structure review — compounding exposure and priority
  • Vendor and contract review — obligations that can be corrected or exited
  • Cash preservation plan — runway extension without new borrowing
  • Sequenced plan — what to stop first, second, and third
03
Recovery Roadmap
A personalised 6 to 24 month plan for getting your business or money back on track. Not a template. Built specifically for your situation — your remaining assets, your tax position, your family obligations, your realistic options. Written in plain language. Specific steps. Specific timelines. Something you can act on without needing it explained.
How the roadmap is built
The roadmap begins from your actual current position — not from where you wished you were. We map what you have left: financial assets, business relationships, skills, obligations, and time. We build a sequence of actions that moves from your current position toward a stable and growing one, accounting for your tax position, family situation, and the constraints of your industry.
Right for you if
The situation is stabilised and you are ready to rebuild. You need something to show your family, your bank, or your next business partner.

What you receive
  • Personalised 6–24 month plan with specific milestones
  • Tax and compliance integration — built around your obligations
  • Capital reallocation guidance — what to do with what you still have
  • Scenario planning — what the plan looks like if conditions change
  • Written in plain language — shareable with family and advisors
04
Guided Re-Entry
Once stable, we walk alongside you as you invest or build again — this time with the guardrails that were missing before. Access to the expert panel before major decisions. Co-investment alongside the founding circle where appropriate. A sounding board that is always honest and never selling anything.
What ongoing support looks like
Re-entry after a serious error is a different experience to first-time entry. The person who has recovered from a real loss brings something that cannot be learned from books: the specific texture of having been wrong in a high-stakes situation and found the way back. They are more patient, more precise, and more honest about what they do not know. Guided Re-Entry is not a product — it is a relationship.
Right for you if
The recovery plan has been followed, the situation has stabilised, and you are ready to deploy capital or build again.

What you receive
  • Ongoing panel access — call on the right expert before any major decision
  • Quarterly review — progress against the roadmap, recalibrated as needed
  • Co-investment access — verified opportunities alongside the founding circle
  • Deal flow — appropriate to your rebuilt position
  • Circle membership — the recovered investor and founder community
The Four Services Connect

Each service is complete in itself. You do not need to commit to all four. But for those who go through the full journey — from diagnosis to re-entry — each stage prepares the ground for the next. Nobody is pushed forward before they are ready.
