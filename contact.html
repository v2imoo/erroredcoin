One email address handles everything — new enquiries, questions about a specific service, and any other reason to get in touch. No triage, no routing, no automated response on the other side.

One address for everything
team@errored.co.in
Write in whatever language is comfortable — English, Hindi, or a mix. Describe your situation in whatever way feels natural. There is no correct format.
What to include in your first email
  • A brief description of what happened — as clearly as you can put it
  • The type of business or investment involved, and roughly what scale
  • How long ago the situation began or the error occurred
  • What you have already tried, if anything
  • What you are hoping to understand or achieve
You do not need to have
  • A clean or complete picture of what went wrong
  • All the financial numbers ready
  • A clear idea of which service you need
  • Certainty that we are the right fit for your situation
Confidentiality
Everything shared with Errored Coin is treated as strictly confidential. The panel members engaged on your situation sign a non-disclosure commitment before any details are shared with them. No case detail or client information is disclosed to any third party without explicit prior consent.
Who responds
Your email is read by a member of the advisory team — not routed through a customer service layer or an automated tool. The person who reads your first email is familiar with the full range of situations Errored Coin handles and can make an initial assessment before responding.
What happens after you write
We respond within one business day. If your situation appears to be within the scope of what we handle, we will ask a small number of follow-up questions. We will tell you honestly at this stage whether we are the right fit or whether a different type of advisory is more appropriate. There is no obligation to proceed further.
If you are unsure whether this is right for you
Write anyway. Describe what happened and what you are uncertain about. We will read it, assess whether it is within our scope, and give you an honest response. We would rather tell you that a different kind of help is more appropriate than take on an engagement where we cannot genuinely add value.

What Happens After You Write

A clear, predictable sequence. No surprises. No pressure. You move forward only when you decide to.

1
You write
One email to team@errored.co.in. Plain language. Describe what happened. No format required.
2
We respond
Within one business day. We confirm scope and ask any clarifying questions needed.
3

A 90-minute Health Check with the relevant panel. In person or on a call. The diagnosis begins.
4

Within a few days — the error named in plain language. Realistic options set out. Your decision to make.
No obligation
Writing to us commits you to nothing. The initial exchange is free. You decide whether to proceed after seeing our response.
Fully confidential
Everything shared is treated as strictly confidential from the first email. No third-party disclosure without your explicit consent.
Honest fit assessment
If we are not the right fit, we will tell you and point you toward what is. We do not take on engagements where we cannot add value.
